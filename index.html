🌊 Flood Safety Guide

Before a Flood

✔ Know your flood risk area
✔ Prepare emergency kit (water, food, torch)
✔ Charge phones & power banks
✔ Keep documents safe
✔ Plan escape routes

During a Flood

⚠ Move to higher ground immediately
⚠ Avoid walking/driving in water
⚠ Stay updated with alerts
⚠ Turn off electricity (if safe)
⚠ Follow emergency instructions

After a Flood

✔ Return only when safe
✔ Avoid dirty/contaminated water
✔ Check home damage
✔ Clean & disinfect everything
✔ Watch for health issues
